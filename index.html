About Us

Padres by Chicago Style brings the heart of Chicago's iconic cuisine to Iloilo City. We specialize in authentic Chicago-style pizzas, crafted with premium ingredients and a commitment to quality. Our menu features a blend of international comfort foods, from deep-dish delights to hearty sandwiches. Enjoy a warm, family-friendly atmosphere where every meal feels like home.
